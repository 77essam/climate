About

Renewal Youth Organization for Development and Peace (RYO) is a youth and women-led organization, founded and officially registered with the Ministry of Social Affairs and Labor in late 2019 by a dedicated team of youth and women, RYO embodies the spirit of resilience and empowerment. We are committed to enabling Yemeni youth and women to become influential voices and active forces in the realms of peace and development within their communities. Through capacity building, advocacy, and strategic partnerships, RYO seeks to foster a more equitable, inclusive, and peaceful society where every individual has the opportunity to thrive.
